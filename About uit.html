UTTRANCHAL UNIVERSITY

UTTRANCHAL UNIVERSITY has been recognized by the UGC under sections 2(f) and 12 (B) of the UGC Act, 1956, and by other statutory bodies of the State and the Nation. The University has been accredited with the prestigious “A+ Grade” by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Uttaranchal University has become the first university in the State of Uttarakhand to receive NAAC A+ Grade in the first cycle of Accreditation, thus creating history. This firmly positions us among the top 5% HEIs of the nation. The university is undeterred in its mission to provide the best experience to all its students whether regional, national or international, through quality teaching, investing in research and innovation.
